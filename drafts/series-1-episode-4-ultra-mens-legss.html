Series 1 Episode 4: Ultra Men's Legss

Will Mathewson, Nathan Ward

Sun 04 October 2020 under Podcast

This episode will be available on iTunes and Spotify.

After taking a phenomenally long time to edit and release their previous episode Will and Nathan are back, channelling their inner Anthony Fantanos to review IDLES latest release, Ultra Mono — a much anticipated album f or the hosts. Did it it live up to the hype? Do they love it, do they hate it? Come and find out.

Will and Nathan also express their opinions about Working Men’s Clubs self-titled debut album which was recently released on Heavenly Recordings after several set backs and delays (which is an exceptional debut record — check it out!). There’s also a look in for Legss latest EP, Doomswayers.
